Our Facilities & Services


Operative Facilities


  • Major Operation Theater: Equipped with advanced surgical equipment for performing critical surgeries.
  • Minor Operation Theater: Designed for minor surgical procedures and day surgeries requiring less intensive resources.
  • Endoscopy Room: Specialized room for performing endoscopic procedures, allowing doctors to diagnose and treat conditions using minimally invasive techniques.

Emergency Rooms (Casualty)


  • 24*7 Emergency Services: Round-the-clock emergency services providing immediate care for urgent medical conditions.
  • Consultation Area: Designated area where patients can consult with doctors for diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Patient Waiting Area: Comfortable and spacious area for patients and their families to wait before consultations and procedures.

Outpatient Services


  • Specialist OPD: Outpatient department where patients can consult with specialist doctors for specific medical conditions.
  • Vaccination: Providing routine vaccinations to protect against various diseases.
  • Health Check Ups: Comprehensive health screenings to monitor overall health and detect any early signs of illness.

Pharmacy


  • 24-Hour Pharmacy Service: Providing patients with access to essential medications and pharmacy services at any time of the day or night.
